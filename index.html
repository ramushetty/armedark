Armed Ark

About Us

Armed Ark is a six piece progressive metal from Hyderabad, India.

The band's current line up consists of guitarist Vijay Paul, Drummer Spurjan Raj, vocalist Vindya naidu, guitarist John Isaac, keyboardist Pradeep neelam and bassist Garry Matthews. ...The band was founded in 2014 by Nishant kumar who has also named the band as Armed Ark. Armed Ark initially started as four piece alternative rock band with vocalist Srimanth Kumar, guitarist Barath kumar, bassist Nishant Kumar and the drummer Manoj kumar. The band was playing regional music in languages Hindi and Telugu. In the year 2014 the band decided to expand the sound going more in the direction of heavy metal. Barath kumar insisted that the band needs another guitarist.Bassist Nishant kumar invited his friend John Isaac who's a guitarist and singer songwriter heavily influenced by the bands like Metallica, Megadeth, Dream theater, Opeth and other thrash and progressive metal bands. The guitarist also suggested to add in a keyboardist and John Isaac invited His good friend Pradeep Neelam to join Armed Ark. The band has already started writing their music in the year 2014 and were making sure the sound was heavy. Soon the band had to look for a guitarist as one of the founding members of the band, Barath kumar decided to quit . The band was impressed by a guitarist named Vijay paul who was also had similar influences as band's guitarist John Isaac, without any delay Armed Ark invited Vijay paul to join them .In the year 2015 the band was all set to write and play some progressive thrash metal music as the two main songwriters John and Vijay were influenced by 80's , 90's thrash metal and progressive metal. The band was evolved from alternative rock to Progressive Thrash metal . Armed Ark continued writing music and playing live . After few months the band was hit by another blow as the drummer Manoj kumar decided to quit the band. Armed Ark found a replacement for Manoj as Spurjan Raj Joined Armed Ark as a Drummer...
